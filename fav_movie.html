CAST

*Notting Hill* (1999) stars Hugh Grant as William "Will" Thacker, a humble London bookstore owner, and Julia Roberts as Anna Scott, a world-famous Hollywood actress. The supporting cast includes Rhys Ifans as Spike, Will's quirky and unforgettable roommate;Emma Chambers as Honey Thacker, Will's cheerful younger sister; Gina McKee as Bella; Hugh Bonneville as Bernie; Tim McInnerny as Max; James Dreyfus as Martin; Richard McCabe as Tony; and Samuel West as Anna's co-star. Together, the cast brings warmth, humor, and charm to this beloved romantic comedy.

STORY

Notting Hill tells the story of William "Will" Thacker, a quiet bookstore owner living in the charming London neighborhood of Notting Hill. His ordinary life takes an unexpected turn when he meets Anna Scott, one of the world's most famous movie stars, who visits his travel bookshop. After a chance encounter and a series of unlikely meetings, the two begin to develop feelings for each other despite coming from completely different worlds.

As their relationship grows, Will and Anna struggle with the pressures of fame, public attention, and their personal insecurities. They experience misunderstandings, heartbreak, and difficult choices before realizing that their love is worth fighting for. In the end, Anna chooses a life with Will, and the film concludes with a heartwarming message that genuine love can overcome differences in status, lifestyle, and circumstance.
